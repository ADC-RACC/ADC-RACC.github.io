What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the ability to be aware of your emotions and what that does to you.

How is EQ different to IQ?

EQ is more closely related to emotional concepts like empathy, motivation, etc. Meanwhile IQ is something

closely related to intellectual properties like memory, and analytical thinking.

Why is EQ important?

EQ is important as an ability to run a sort of self checkup and awareness of self. Then you can analyse your situation and make better changes like self babysitting.
