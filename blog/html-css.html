Margin, Border, and Padding

What is the difference?

13/06/2024

So think of an image as a gobstopper. You have your yummy content in the middle, your padding as a layer above,

your border as the colour line above that, and the margin as the outer crust layer.

starting from the center:

  • Content is the thing you are adding to a site, be it text or image, etc.

  • Padding is just space inbetween your content and border, so you can size the border to any size.

  • Border the sort of perimiter of the content. It could be a solid color, or invisible.

  • Margin The space outside of the Border, Like the atmosphere, giving a lack of space between content.
