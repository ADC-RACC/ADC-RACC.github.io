My Identity and Values

14/06/2024

Identify and name the values most important to you:

Humour, Perspective, Judgment, Hope, Social Intelligence.

Stories of how these values were demonstrated in practice:

When playing games I often know when I am going to lose, but I don’t stop playing, ‘cause giving up when the rest of your team is fighting would be cringe.

A testimonial/feedback from someone:

“Well when we play Barotrauma (cooperative submarine alien game), you always seem to make good calls and put people to work when they’re not sure what to do.”

Achieved Junior Black Belt in Kenpo Karate when I was 15.

Visuals or narratives that help you describe where your values came from:

When I turned 18 I received a framed list of what It means to be a man of the Clark family. I resonated a lot with it because it was what I was already doing haha.

Whakataukī (proverbs) or quotes that sum up your values:

“We must laugh in the face of our helplessness against the forces of nature – or go insane.”

― Charlie Chaplin, My Autobiography

Your review of learning:

I like making/keeping an environment where people can laugh and crack jokes
